IT lead to unemployment in many areas such as:
Manufacturing - robots replace workers in factories, for example in car manufacturing factories the robots replace workers who assemble the car.

Logistic companies - robots now sort out the packages which do not need people to be present on site.

However because of ICT new jobs have also been created: Software Programming Build, designing, and maintaining the robot Other internet related jobs

Although because of IT many jobs have been replaced by AI intelligence, but IT has also created the opportunity for new jobs to arise.
