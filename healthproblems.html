Here are some potential health problems:

  • Repetitive Strain Injury

    This is when the joints like shoulders, wrist or fingers starts hurting. This can lead to hand/arm immobility.
    Causes Prevention
    Typing for extended periods of time Take breaks
    Clickling on mouse rapidly for extended periods of time Use a wrist rest
    Working in a cramped space Tidy up/clear up work space

  • Back Problems

    Pain in the back.
    Causes Preventions
    Sitting with poor posture Use chairs with adjustable seats that has good back support
    Not sitting upright Force yourself to sit upright

  • Eye Problems

    Eyes start to feel dry, or headaches might occur.
    Causes Preventions
    Staring at the computer monitor for extened periods of time Take a break
    Working in a room with poor room lighting Make sure to work in a bright place
    Computer screen glare Use anti-glare screen protectors
